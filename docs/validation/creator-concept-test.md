# Creator Concept Test — send to 6–8 creators

## The opener (DM / message)

> Hey [name] — quick one, and totally fine to ignore.
>
> A close friend of mine (UGC creator) got her Instagram hacked a couple weeks ago and *still* can't get it back. Watching her go through it made me realise how much of your whole business lives inside an account you don't actually own.
>
> I'm thinking about building something to fix that, and I'd really value your gut reaction — takes 2 mins, and I want you to be brutally honest, not nice. Mind reading the below?

---

## What it is (the part they read)

**The problem:** your income, your audience, and your brand deals all live inside an account someone else controls. If it gets hacked or suspended, you lose your only way to reach your followers *and* your brand partners overnight — and there's no reliable way to get it back.

**What I'm building (working name: a safety net for your account).** It doesn't try to recover a hacked account — nobody can honestly promise that. Instead it makes *losing* your account survivable:

1. **Your own backup audience** — a simple link-in-bio page that quietly collects your followers' emails, so you always have a way to reach them that no platform can take away.
2. **A "break glass" button** — if you ever get hacked, one tap emails your whole list and puts up a page saying *"this is my real account, ignore anyone messaging you as me"* — so your brands and followers don't get scammed while you're locked out.
3. **Content backup** — automatically saves your posts and your growth history.
4. **Instant hack alert** — warns you the second someone tries to change your login, so you can act in the minutes that matter.

**Price: $19/month.**

---

## The questions (please be brutally honest)

1. Has anything like this — hacked, locked out, or impersonated — ever happened to **you or someone you know**? What happened?
2. On a scale of 1–10, how much does "if I lose my account, I lose my business" actually worry you day to day?
3. Reading the above — is your honest reaction **"yes, I need that"** or **"meh"**? Why?
4. Would you pay **$19/month** for it? If not $19, what — if anything — would you actually pay?
5. Which **one** feature above matters most to you? And is anything missing that would make it a no-brainer?

Thank you 🙏 — your honest answer helps more than a polite one.
